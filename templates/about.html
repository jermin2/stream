South Pacific Ministry and Fellowship

We present this magazine to our readers with content helpful for all Christians seeking to advance in the normal Christian faith and life, and also in the practice of the normal church life.

Our commitment is to present the basic and solid truths of the Bible to nourish and strengthen all of God’s children.

We hope that this publication will strengthen and supply each believer and be a valuable resource for shepherding the Lord’s children.

We welcome any feedback or questions. If you or a friend would like to receive future issues, write to us at info@streamsouthpacific.org.nz.

We again commit this publication to the Lord, and ask for His blessing, that it might be used by Him unto the building up of His Body and the fulfillment of His eternal purpose.

The Editors
